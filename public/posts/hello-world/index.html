Why Hugo?

Because sometimes you want your blog posts to be plain text files that you can read in any editor, version control with git, and know will still be readable in 20 years.

Also it’s fast. Like, absurdly fast. No database, no PHP, just pure unadulterated shenanigans rendered to HTML.
